ABON KEPITING

If 'abon' is usually made from beef or chicken, this one is made from crab meat. Unlike 'abon sapi', which is shredded in large shreds, this one is actually a little smooth in texture with a slightly pale brownish color.

MANTAU

Mantou, often referred to as Chinese steamed bun, is a type of soft, white steamed bun. Mantau is a kind of unfilled bread that looks like bakpau but has no filling. Mantau can be served fried or steamed.

AMPLANG

Amplang is a traditional snack/cracker made from fish. Amplang is made from mackerel fish, which is mixed with starch and other ingredients, and then fried. It comes in many shapes and forms depends on the factory.
