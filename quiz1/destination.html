DANAU CERMIN

Lake Cermin in Balikpapan is not a real lake, but an artificial lake. But the water is clear, the water is able to reflect objects such as glass. This greenish lake water forms a shadow of trees, boats, the sky and whatever is above it looks beautiful.

BUKIT BATU DINDING

Bukit batu dinding are steep cliffs resembling natural-made walls. The distance that must be taken to the top is approximately 8 kilometers. As far as the eye can see, on both sides of the trekking path are towering cliffs similar to walls.

BUKIT BANGKIRAI

Bukit Bangkirai is a tourist attraction for tropical rainforests in East Kalimantan. This tourist area has tropical rainforests that are still very natural. The trees that grow in this forest are mostly Bangkirai trees which have lived for more than 150 years.
